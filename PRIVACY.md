# Privacy Policy for Kokila TTS

**Effective Date:** July 19, 2026

## 1. Introduction
Kokila TTS ("we", "our", or "the App") is an offline neural Text-to-Speech (TTS) engine. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains our data practices.

## 2. Data Collection and Usage
Kokila TTS operates **100% offline** on your device.
- **No Text Data Collection:** We do not collect, store, or transmit any text you process through the app. All text-to-speech synthesis happens locally on your hardware.
- **No Telemetry:** We do not track your usage, location, or device identifiers.
- **No Third-Party Sharing:** Since we collect no data, we share no data with third parties.

## 3. Network Access
The App only requests network access for the explicit purpose of downloading optional voice models from public repositories (such as GitHub) when you choose to do so within the app.

## 4. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

## 5. Contact Us
If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, please contact the developer via the repository issue tracker.
